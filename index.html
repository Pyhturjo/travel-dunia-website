📋 Required Documents

Tourist Visa — Service Holder

  • Scan copy of passport with minimum 07 month validity
  • Two copies 3.5cm × 4.5cm photo — white background
  • Bank statement with min. 1 Lac Tk closing balance (last 6 months)
  • Bank solvency — balance must match statement
  • Last 6 month salary pay slip (if account is not salary account)
  • NOC letter
  • Office ID card
  • Visiting card

Tourist Visa — Business Person

  • Scan copy of passport with minimum 07 month validity
  • Two copies 3.5cm × 4.5cm photo — white background
  • Bank statement with min. 1 Lac Tk closing balance (last 6 months)
  • Bank solvency — balance must match statement
  • Updated Trade License (English translated & notarized)
  • NOC on company letterhead pad
  • Three page blank company letterhead pad
  • Business card

Tourist Visa — Dependents

  • Scan copy of passport with minimum 07 month validity
  • Two copies 3.5cm × 4.5cm photo — white background
  • Bank statement with min. 1 Lac Tk closing balance (last 6 months)
  • Bank solvency — balance must match statement
  • Marriage certificate or proof of relationship (English translated & notarized)

Tourist Visa — Minors

  • Scan copy of passport with minimum 07 month validity
  • Two copies 3.5cm × 4.5cm photo — white background
  • Bank statement with min. 1 Lac Tk closing balance — last 6 months (cost bearer)
  • Bank solvency — balance must match statement
  • Birth certificate and parental consent (English translated & notarized)

Medical Visa — Service Holder / Business

  • Scan copy of passport with minimum 07 month validity
  • Two copies 3.5cm × 4.5cm photo — white background
  • Bank statement with min. 1 Lac Tk closing balance (last 6 months)
  • Bank solvency — balance must match statement
  • Official invitation/appointment letter from Thai hospital or medical facility
  • Referral letter from local doctor / medical report
  • NOC letter
  • Office ID card / Trade License (for business persons)
  • Visiting card / Business card
